Impulse Resistor High Voltage Impulse Resistor

Carona Power is engaged in manufacturing and supply of the high voltage impulse resistor. A High Voltage Impulse Resistor is a specialized resistor designed shape the impulse wave and accurately measure the same.

Applications

  • Used in generation of standard Lightning Impulses 1.2/50 µs
  • Used in generation or standard Switching Impulses 250/2500 µs
  • Used in generation of standard lightning and switching current impulses of 4/10, 8/20 µs
  • Used in generation of non-standard impulses voltages & currents
  • Used in generation of oscillatory impulse voltages
  • Used as a wave damping resistor
  • Used as a discharge resistor for energy storage capacitors
  • Used in measurement of impulses as resistive dividers
  • Used as a charging resistor to limit the capacitor charging current
  • Used as a protection resistor to discharge the energy stored in impulse generator
