High Voltage Impulse Inductors

Carona Power is engaged in manufacturing and supply of air cored the high voltage impulse inductor.High Voltage Impulse Inductor is a specialized inductor designed to shape the impulse wave during transformer testing and Lightning arrester testing used in generation of oscillatory impulse in research and development.

Applications

  • Used in shaping the lightning impulse wave in high MVA transformer testing
  • Used in Glaninger Circuit
  • Used in generation of lightning & switching current impulse for Lightning Arrester testing
  • Used in generation of oscillatory impulse generation
  • Used in research in development in generation of non-standard oscillatory impulse waves for GIS systems.
