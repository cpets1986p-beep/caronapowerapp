Carona Power Electrical Test Systems is a engineering firm specializing in High Voltage Impulse Generator service, repair, refurbishment, and spares support. With deep industry experience in impulse voltage and current testing systems, Carona Power supports customers who depend on reliable operation of high voltage test equipment for routine testing, type testing, and R&D applications. We at Carona Power Electrical Test Systems have extensive hands-on experience in High Voltage Test and Measurement Equipment, gained through years of work with established test system manufacturers. The company was formed to address a critical industry needs, especially for systems that are aging, unsupported, or require expert intervention.

Core Technical Focus Areas

  • Servicing of High Voltage Impulse Voltage Generators
  • Servicing of High Voltage Impulse Current Generators
  • Design & Manufacturing of Wave-shaping Resistor, Inductor Networks
  • Performance Verification of HV Source & Measurement System
  • Service, Refurbishment of Legacy Systems
  • Upgrade of Existing Impulse Test Equipment
  • High Voltage Laboratory Grounding Techniques
  • HV Laboratory Planning Consultation
  • PD Room Construction
  • Training Operators for Routine Testing
  • Educating Customer for Best Practises

Carona Power combines field experience, practical troubleshooting skills, and engineering insight to deliver effective and reliable solutions.
