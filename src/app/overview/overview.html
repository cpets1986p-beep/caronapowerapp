Impulse Generator Service & Repair

Carona Power offers professional service and repair support for High Voltage Impulse Generators, helping customers restore system performance and extend equipment life.


Current service capabilities include:

  • Service support for W.S Test Systems (WSTS) supplied equipments
  • Service support for WSTS supplied Passoni & Villa (P&V) Type Impulse Generators
  • Service support for MWB GmbH Impulse Generator
  • Service support for Other multimake OEM impulse generators
  • Service support for Custom-built and modified impulse generators
  • Electrical and mechanical fault diagnosis
  • Replacement of failed impulse components
  • System testing and waveform verification after repair

Our service approach emphasizes safety, reliability, and compliance with impulse testing requirements.

Customers may contact us with system details for technical evaluation and support feasibility.
